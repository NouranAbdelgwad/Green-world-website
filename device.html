Introducing Smart Green Assistant: A Sustainable Solution for Gardening and Agriculture

INTRO The Smart Green Assistant is a revolutionary device designed to read temperature, humidity, and soil moisture levels, making it an invaluable tool for gardeners and farmers alike.

SUSTAINABILITY What sets this device apart is its focus on sustainability, as it is constructed entirely from recycled materials and offered at an affordable price, making it accessible to individuals from all walks of life.

DESIGN With its intuitive design and user-friendly interface, the Smart Green Assistant provides accurate and reliable data to help users optimize their gardening or farming practices, resulting in healthier plants, higher yields, and reduced environmental impact. By empowering individuals to make informed decisions about their land use.

LAST the Smart Green Assistant is helping to build a more sustainable future for all.

About Us

we are students at WE school for applied technology, Our project aims to help people lead an environmentally conscious lifestyle effortlessly, focusing on water and agriculture. We're developing an AI-powered app with a smart camera to monitor plant growth, a social media platform, and a smart product to test the soil state. We plan to organize a campaign to exchange non-biodegradable waste for eco-friendly rewards.
